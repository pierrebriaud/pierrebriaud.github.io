Since November 2025, I am a CNRS researcher at XLIM in the University of Limoges, and a member of the Cryptis team. I am interested in algebraic cryptanalysis, applied to various constructions.

Previously, from 2024 to 2025, I was a post-doctoral researcher in the Cryptography department of Simula UiB. Prior to that, from 2020 to 2023, I was a PhD student in the COSMIQ project-team of Inria Paris, under the supervision of Jean-Pierre Tillich.
